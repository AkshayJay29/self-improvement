Why join us?

Your heart will thank you!

Going to the gym improves aerobic fitness, and so boosts your cardiovascular health. It also builds muscle and burns calories which keeps you in great shape.

Be the strongest

The gym and boxing sessions work together to greatly strenghen your heart by pumping blood more effectively through your body.

Feel the best

Physical exercise in the gym, as well as boxing, release remarkable hormones, such as serotonin and endorphins, in the brain. Serotonin helps stabilize your mood and well-being. It helps other systems in your body function as well. Endorphins make you more energetic, more awake and, yes, happier.

Become a social king

Physical exercise releases 'feel good' hormones in the brain, which boosts your self - confidence. This enhances your social skills, and makes you feel like an absolute boss in the room. When we train with others, we can train harder more easily. Social training can pull you out of a 'gym demotivation' or low mood, and inspire you to make new friends with a similar mindset as you.
